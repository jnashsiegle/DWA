Examples of Work - The Good, The Bad and The Ugly

These examples range from school projects to hobbies, photography, photomanipulation, programmming code and various front-end|back-end frameworks. My opinion on frameworks is that they have their place but nothing replaces knowing how to do it from scratch to begin with. Strengthening programming foundations in HTML/CSS, JavaScript, PHP, AJAX, JSON, Laravel, AngularJS are ongoing. I jest with the title as don't we all look back at what we once thought was good and think "how could I have ever thought that was good?" This is my starting point from two years ago forward. The beginning portfolio of a web developer striving to learn all that she can to offer the world all that is within her.

There are links to the websites themselves, links to their respective github repositories to see the progression of the assignment and source codes and as well links to any extra files that were developed such as audio or video files.

As well, below on deviantart and behance there are more image files of graphics and photos that I have created over the last 3-4 years. While I am not a graphic artist I do enjoy it as a hobby, more in the realm of photomanipulations and black and white photography. While I am not a designer persay, I do maintain the basic design principles and have an eye to emulate ideas that are given.

Web Development to me is like a massive pile of puzzle pieces. It is up to me to sort them out much like the ideas and needs of a client, the tools needed by each puzzle to guide it to a mode of completion. Then seeing how many puzzles I have before me the fun begins with actually utilizing the toolbox before me as my eyes watch the glorious transformation of an idea, or pile of pieces become an actual visual and interactive vision that literally reaches around the world.
